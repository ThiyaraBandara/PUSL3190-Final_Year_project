About the System

Our Phishing Detection System is a user-friendly tool designed to help you identify and avoid phishing websites. By analyzing URLs and their content, the system assigns a phishing score and provides a detailed report to keep you informed and secure.

How it works:

  • Enter a website URL to scan.
  • The system crawls the site and analyzes links for phishing characteristics.
  • Each link is scored and categorized:
  • Phishing Site: Score 70+ (red)
  • Maybe a Phishing Site: Score 31-69 (black)
  • Not a Phishing Site: Score 0-30 (green)

Receive a report by email and stay protected from online threats!

Our system is designed to be intuitive and easy to use, ensuring that even those with minimal technical knowledge can protect themselves from phishing attacks. The tool is constantly updated to keep up with the latest phishing techniques, providing you with the most accurate and up-to-date protection.

We are committed to your online safety and continuously strive to improve our system based on user feedback and the latest cybersecurity trends.
